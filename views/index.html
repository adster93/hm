Getting Started

First, you’ll need to purchase Bitcoin mining hardware. To even have a chance at any kind of profit, your best bet is to purchase an Antminer S7 or an Avalon6 since they are the most effecient miners.

If you just want to play around with mining, grab a USB miner to learn the basics at a small startup cost.
